Welcome to Tennis at Cal!
We are Cal's recreational tennis organization - check us out!
Who We Are
We're a group of fun-loving individuals that love playing tennis! Our purpose is to provide opportunities for UC Berkeley members to compete and socialize with students and faculty who have an interest in tennis at the Berkeley campus.

Practices

Our practices are on Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday. Monday and Wednesday practices go from 7-9 PM, and Saturday practices go from 2-4 PM. All practices are on Hearst Courts.

Practices are not mandatory - feel free to come and go as you please!

Competition and Tournaments

When you join TAC, you will be placed into a division that contains players with a skill level similar to yours, so you will always play with someone that is around your competitive level.

Additionally, we host competitive events throughout the semester. We have a singles and doubles tournament. We also have a World Team Tennis tournament near the end of every semester as well.

Socials

We host multiple socials throughout the semester to help our members get to know each other!

In the past, we have gone ice-skating, held board-game nights, and went bowling. We also have a club tradition of going to Fenton's Creamery in Oakland every semester! We also sometimes take trips to see the Cal Men's and Women's Tennis Team in action.
